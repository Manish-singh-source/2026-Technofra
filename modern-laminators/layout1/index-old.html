About Us

Modern Laminators – Excellence in Flexible Packaging

Looking for the perfect flexible packaging laminating solution for your products? Then, you have come to the right place! A leading manufacturer of flexible packing laminates, Modern Laminators has developed a varied expertise during forty years of service to both Indian and foreign companies in the fields of food, FMCG, agricultural and pharmaceutical products.

We pride ourselves on using ecological-sound and best-quality raw materials that give the required exposure to your products. Contact us and our experts will advise you on the best packaging to preserve your goods and impress your customers. We can add a host of features to bring the gloss out of your final product.
